They Disguise A Bible As The Quran. Watch How People React To These Horrible “Quran” Passages

Advertisement
2.5K Shares
Jenny Brown
December 15th, 2015

With the terrorist attacks in Paris linked to ISIS and the recent mass shooting in San Bernardino by two Muslims, many Americans have become more and more fearful. Anti-Muslim sentiments have become more common and blatant in the media, and it’s gradually growing out of control.

Many individuals are criticizing Muslims for their beliefs that stem from their version of a bible, the Quran. They assume that the justification for the violence is embedded within the religion. They state that the Islam religion teaches its followers to lash out violently, which makes it possible for any Muslim to be a terrorist.

Advertisement

While this may sound preposterous to many of us (most of us know better), there are still some who do not get it. Thankfully, some Dutch pranksters carried out a social experiment to prove an important point.

They took a Bible and disguised it to look like a Quran.

Then they searched through the Bible to find passages that many Westerners would disagree with or question.

Some passages include:

  • “If you reject my commands and abhor my laws, you will eat the flesh of your own sons. And your own daughters.”
  • “A woman should live in quietness and full submission.”
  • “If two men sleep with each other, they will both have to be killed.”

They found strangers and told them these passages… They were shocked by what they heard.

They became judgmental of the Islam faith.

But when they learned the truth? They’re shocked.

The moral of the story? As society changes, so do our views, opinions, and religions. Just like how many Christians do not follow every word in the Bible, Muslims don’t follow every word in the Quran.

We should keep this in perspective when talking about other religions.
