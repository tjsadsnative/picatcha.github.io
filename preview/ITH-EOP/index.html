Film Session: Alcorn State

Like
Tweet

Indiana routed Alcorn State on Monday night thanks to a 33-point performance from James Blackmon Jr.

We’ll take a look at two of his scores as well as an Indiana turnover and two defensive possessions for the Hoosiers in the latest edition of Film Session:

Tamarcio Wilson gets Blackmon Jr. leaning right with a move and takes him left:

He throw a spin move on him in the lane to try and create more space:

Blackmon Jr. gets a little behind, but sticks with the play and is able to strip the ball:

He starts bringing the ball up the court:

And finds Robert Johnson coming down the right wing:

Johnson gives it right back to Blackmon Jr.:

He scores, is fouled and completes the 3-point play at the line:

A nice job turning defensive into offense by Blackmon Jr. here, showing all his points didn’t just come via his teammates or 3-pointers.
