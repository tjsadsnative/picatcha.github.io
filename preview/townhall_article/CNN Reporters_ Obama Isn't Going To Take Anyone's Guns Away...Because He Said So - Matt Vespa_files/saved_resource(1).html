The Federal Law on gun sales (transfers) is "In regard to transferring firearms between individuals residing in the same state, any person may sell a firearm to an unlicensed resident of the State where he resides as long as he or she does not know or have reasonable cause to believe the person is prohibited from receiving or possessing firearms under Federal law." And "All persons who transfer firearms, regardless of whether they are engaged in the business of dealing in firearms, must ensure that any transfers are in compliance with federal, state and local laws."

There is no universal registration system for guns in the US. A background check is not for the gun but for checking if the person buying the gun can legally do so. And it only required for people who sell guns as a business.
