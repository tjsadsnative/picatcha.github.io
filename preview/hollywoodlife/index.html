WOW. Where has this Justin Bieber bop been all of our lives? Wait until you hear ‘I’ll Be There’ — it’s AMAZING.

Justin Bieber, 21, has been hiding a real gem from us. A previously unreleased track, “I’ll Be There,” has just leaked online and it’s a total banger.

Are you ready for this? A seriously hot new song from Justin Bieber is going viral online, and once you hear it you’ll know why — “I’ll Be There” is bop central. The song, which has a nice R&B flow along with Justin showing off his rap skills, is a total hit. Unfortunately, it was never released — though it would have sounded great on his latest album, Purpose, which you can download HERE.

It’s hard to tell when “I’ll Be There” was recorded. It would have definitely fit well on 2012’s Believe, wouldn’t it?
