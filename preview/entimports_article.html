“UFC FN 86” Bonus Report: No FOTN, 4 Fighters Take Home $50,000 POTN Bonuses

April 10, 2016

“UFC Fight Night 86” is in the books and for those fans who were able to catch the entire card were treated to an amazing day of fights!

During the post fight press-conference, they revealed that instead of handing out two Performance of the Night (POTN) bonuses and a Fight of the Night (FOTN) bonus, they would be dishing out FOUR POTN bonuses!

Jared Cannonier (Performance of the Night)
Fans who are subscribed to “UFC Fight Pass” were treated to an insane knockout in the second fight of the night. Forget a cup of coffee— Jared Cannonier will wake you up by putting someone else to sleep! He sent Cyril Asker tumbling to the canvas in just under three minutes and set an exciting pace for all fighters to follow.

Alejandro Perez (Performance of the Night)
Alejandro Perez had no issues following the pace set by Jared Cannonier. Before Perez could follow up with shots to knock Ian Entwistle, Entwistle actually verbally submitted! Perez raked in an extra $50,000 for just four minutes worth of fighting. Not too bad.

Derrick Lewis (Performance of the Night)
Derrick “Black Beast” Lewis continues to impress in the heavyweight division. He knew a knockout at “UFC Fight Night 86” against a veteran like Gabriel Gonzaga would hush any naysayers; Lewis needed just one round to drop Gonzaga and finish him off. It was a scary moment in the octagon after the bout, actually, when Gonzaga started shaking. He made his way back to his feet, though, and is recovered.

Mairbek Taisumov (Performance of the Night)
Damir Hadzovic fell fast as soon as Mairbek Taisumov’s uppercut connected in the fourth minute of their preliminary fight. Lightweight Taisumov is in a division that is stacked to the gills and knew he needed to make a big impact if he wanted to get ahead of other big names in the rankings. Will Taisumov make a big move on Monday evening?
