Everyone's Going Nuts Over This Hair Tutorial

The One Hairstyle All The Celebs Are Freaking Out Over Is Literally The Easiest Hairdo Ever

Dimitrios Kambouris/Getty Images Entertainment/Getty Images
Melanie Richtman
4 hours ago Fashion & Beauty

You've probably seen your favorite celebs rocking a high ponytail on the red carpet. Chrissy Teigen, Gigi Hadid, and basically all of the Kardashians are just a few of the names who have been wearing this high pony out and about. However, the new name for it was coined by Jen Atkin, who is arguably the most famous hairstylist of the moment (thanks to her official position with the Kardashians). She's calling the high ponytail the "ditzy ponytail" and everyone's weirdly obsessed with it.

Although I don't agree with the name, the hairstyle is super cute. Basically, to achieve the "ditzy ponytail," you put your hair up in a high ponytail (like I've been doing for years, ever since I had the motor skills to successfully put a hair tie around my hair more than once), tug and pull, and voila. Jen Atkin even put out a tutorial for the hairdo that goes along with her new hair product line, Ouai, and it's kind of the best thing ever, assuming you have a sense of humor that rivals mine.

If you're not a high ponytail connoisseur, allow me to break down the steps for you. Step 1: Put your hair in a high pony. This is really the only step needed, but since this is to promote the Ouai line, they added a second step, which is to spray your hair with dry shampoo.

Pretty self explanatory, right?
